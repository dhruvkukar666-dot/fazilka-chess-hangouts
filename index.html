FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

I don't know how to play chess. Can I still come?+
Absolutely! We have a "learn chess in 10 minutes" corner at every meetup. Half the fun is learning over coffee. You'll be moving pieces in no time.
Is there a fee to attend?+
Zero. Nada. Nothing. Just buy your own coffee or snack at the café if you'd like — that's how we keep the café happy hosting us.
Do I need to bring my own chess board?+
We have boards! The Instagram post says "bring a chess set if possible" — if you own one, bring it along. The more the merrier.
I'm not good enough to play with others.+
There's genuinely no "good enough" here. No ratings, no rankings, no pressure. We pair you with someone at your level. Everyone is here to have fun.
How do I find out where the next meetup is?+
Join our WhatsApp group! Every week we announce the café venue there. It's the single best way to stay in the loop.
Can I bring a friend who has never played?+
That's literally our favourite thing. Every event ends with "bring one friend next week." The more new faces, the better the community becomes.
